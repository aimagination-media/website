This Privacy Policy describes how AImagination Studio ("we", "us") handles information when you visit aimaginationx.com (the "Site"). We are committed to keeping your data safe and being transparent about how it is used.

The Site embeds videos and content from YouTube and links to third-party platforms (Patreon, Instagram, TikTok). When you interact with that embedded content, the provider (e.g., YouTube/Google) may set cookies and collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Cookies

We use only minimal cookies necessary for the Site to function. Third-party embeds may set their own cookies; you can manage these through your browser settings or the provider's preferences.

How We Use Information

  • To understand how the Site is used and improve it
  • To maintain and protect the security of the Site
  • To understand which content resonates with viewers

Data Retention

We do not maintain profiles of individual visitors. Any analytics data we rely on is retained in aggregate form only, for as long as needed for the purposes described above.

Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., under the GDPR or CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data. As we do not collect personal data directly through the Site, these rights generally relate to the third-party platforms we link to.
